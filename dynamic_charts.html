Dynamic Visualizations

Below are interactive charts for exploring credit risk relationships dynamically:

Health and Income by Region

This chart allows users to explore the relationship between age, income, and loan intent with an interactive range selection.

Health and Income by Region (Radio Filter)

Use the radio buttons to filter data by loan intent and observe its impact on the relationship between age and income.

Loan Grade Counts

This bar chart shows the distribution of loan grades, allowing analysis of financial risk profiles by grade.
