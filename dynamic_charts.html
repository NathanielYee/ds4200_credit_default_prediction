About

In this section, we dive deeper into our analysis of credit risk, focusing on the relationships between key factors such as income levels, loan amounts, and credit history. Our findings suggest a strong correlation between financial variables and the probability of default, underscoring the importance of robust data preprocessing and model selection in assessing creditworthiness.

  • Probability of Default: The likelihood that a borrower will fail to meet their debt obligations.
  • Loss Given Default: The amount of loss a lender incurs if a borrower defaults.
  • Exposure at Default: The total value exposed to risk at the time of default.
