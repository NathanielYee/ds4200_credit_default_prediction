About This Project

This project explores the use of machine learning techniques, data preprocessing, and statistical analysis to assess and predict credit risk. It aims to highlight key financial and demographic factors influencing credit default risk, providing insights valuable to financial institutions and researchers. By analyzing a dataset of over 32,000 entries with variables such as income, loan amount, and credit history, we aim to model repayment likelihood and identify trends that can assist in managing credit risk effectively.
