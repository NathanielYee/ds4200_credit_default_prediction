About This Project

This project explores the use of machine learning techniques, data preprocessing, and statistical analysis to assess and predict credit risk. It aims to highlight key financial and demographic factors influencing credit default risk, providing insights valuable to financial institutions and researchers.

Project Goals and Motivation

The goal of this project is to demonstrate proficiency in data visualization and analysis techniques by exploring a dataset of over 32,000 entries. By analyzing variables such as income, loan amount, and credit history, we aim to:

  • Model repayment likelihood and predict credit default risk.
  • Identify trends that assist in managing credit risk effectively.
  • Explore and visualize relationships between demographic and financial factors.

Dataset Overview

The dataset includes over 32,000 observations with 12 variables, such as:

  • Person Age: The age of the borrower.
  • Loan Amount: The total amount borrowed.
  • Person Income: The annual income of the borrower.
  • Loan Intent: The purpose of the loan (e.g., debt consolidation, education).
  • Loan Status: Whether the borrower defaulted or repaid.
